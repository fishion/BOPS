Our Vision

BOPS are a small team of Hangleton based creative thinkers and doers looking to improve our area and bring some much needed colour, joy and inspiration to the streets of our suburb.

We want to engage the local and wider community with visual arts that everyone can enjoy.

Our Approach

The ‘love local, enjoy local’ aesthetic is now even more important than ever before, promoting this concept and giving something back to the local community benefits everyone.

Working with local Artists and Hangleton & Knoll businesses to create permenant art installations and trails that aim to improve and uplift the suburb streets. Street furniture, signage and spaces for leisure can all be enhanced by colour.

The Team

Melanie Sramek-Bennett is a freelance Graphic Designer and Art Director with 20+ years experience bringing brands to life.

I believe in using the visual arts to inspire, uplift and improve the world around us. I believe that great design and unexpected art should be accessible to all and can have huge positive benefits for the community. Alongside design for the commercial world I also created a Snail for the Wild in Art SnailTrail, as well as working with children at two local Primarys’ on their Snow Dog projects.

Nicole Monney has over 25 years' experience working in the voluntary sector in positions that have spanned frontline delivery through to programme coordination, chief executive and consultancy roles; with a long history of establishing multi-agency and cross sector partnerships.

Holding qualifications in Youth & Community Work and International Health Promotion, social justice and social change are guiding principles.
